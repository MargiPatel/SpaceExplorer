Privacy Policy for Educational Space Exploration App

Last Updated: March 12, 2026
Effective Date: March 12, 2026

🛡️ Privacy First: Space Explorer is designed with children's privacy as our top priority. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data stays on your device.

📖 1. Introduction

Welcome to Space Explorer, an educational space exploration app designed for children aged 6-12 years old. This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to protecting your child's privacy and describes how our app handles data.

Space Explorer is developed by Margi Patel and is intended to provide a safe, educational, and fun learning experience about space, planets, and astronomy.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 2. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Space Explorer is designed specifically for children and fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Our Commitment:

  • No Personal Information Collection: We do not collect any personally identifiable information from children or adults.
  • No Account Registration: The app does not require creating an account, providing an email address, or any form of registration.
  • No Advertising: Space Explorer contains no third-party advertising or tracking.
  • No In-App Purchases: There are no purchases, subscriptions, or paid features that require payment information.
  • No Third-Party Data Sharing: We do not share any data with third parties.

📱 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

To be completely transparent, here is what Space Explorer does NOT collect:

  • ❌ Names or usernames (profile names are stored locally only)
  • ❌ Email addresses
  • ❌ Phone numbers
  • ❌ Physical addresses
  • ❌ Photographs or videos of users
  • ❌ Location data or GPS information
  • ❌ Device identifiers (UDID, advertising IDs)
  • ❌ IP addresses
  • ❌ Browsing history or usage analytics
  • ❌ Contact lists
  • ❌ Social media information
  • ❌ Payment or credit card information

💾 4. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Space Explorer stores certain information locally on your device only to provide the app's educational features. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server or third party.

Local Data Includes:

  1. Profile Information:
    • Profile name chosen by the user (stored locally using UserDefaults)
    • Avatar selection
    • Age range (for age-appropriate content)
  2. Game Progress:
    • Stars earned
    • Quiz scores for different topics (Atmosphere, Planets, Deep Space)
    • Achievement tiers (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum)
    • Activity completion status
  3. App Preferences:
    • Audio settings
    • Whether the user has completed onboarding
    • Last selected profile

🔒 Important: All this data is stored using iOS's standard UserDefaults system, which keeps data securely on the device in your app's private storage area. No one else has access to this data, not even us as the developers.

🎵 5. Audio Features

Space Explorer includes audio narration features to enhance the educational experience:

  • Text-to-Speech: The app uses iOS's built-in AVSpeechSynthesizer to narrate educational content. This happens entirely on your device with no internet connection required.
  • Audio Files: Pre-recorded audio narration files are included in the app bundle. No audio is recorded, transmitted, or stored outside the app.
  • Microphone Access: Space Explorer does NOT access your device's microphone and does not record any audio.

🌐 6. Internet Connectivity

Space Explorer is designed to work completely offline and does not require an internet connection:

  • ✅ All content, images, and animations are included in the app
  • ✅ No data is sent to or received from external servers
  • ✅ The app functions fully in airplane mode
  • ✅ No analytics or tracking services are used

🔐 7. Data Security

While Space Explorer does not collect or transmit personal data, we still take security seriously:

  • Sandboxed Storage: All local data is stored in the app's sandboxed container, protected by iOS security mechanisms.
  • No Cloud Sync: Currently, data is not synced to iCloud or any cloud service, ensuring it remains only on your device.
  • App Deletion: If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device.

🔗 8. Third-Party Services

Space Explorer does NOT use any third-party services, including:

  • ❌ Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
  • ❌ Advertising networks
  • ❌ Social media integration
  • ❌ Crash reporting services
  • ❌ Cloud storage providers (except for potential future optional iCloud sync)

Third-Party Frameworks: The app uses the following open-source libraries that run locally on your device:

  • Lottie by Airbnb: For displaying animations (runs locally, no network access)

👨‍👩‍👧 10. Parental Controls

Parents and guardians have full control over their child's use of Space Explorer:

  • Profile Management: Parents can create, edit, or delete user profiles at any time.
  • Progress Reset: Profile data can be reset by deleting and recreating a profile.
  • Complete Data Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all local data permanently.
  • No External Communication: The app has no messaging, chat, or social features.

🔄 11. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

To Delete a Specific Profile:

  1. Open Space Explorer
  2. Tap on edit icon on your profile card
  3. Tap the delete option at the end of the pop up screen

To Delete All Data:

  1. Uninstall the Space Explorer app from your device
  2. All locally stored data will be permanently removed

⚠️ Note: Since no data is stored on our servers, we cannot recover deleted data. Make sure you want to delete before proceeding.
